Isle of Skye Highland Games

Base: Glengarry Castle Hotel · leave ~6:45–7 AM · dinner booked in Colbost — very late return

  • By 9:30 AM
    Skye Highland Games — The Lump, Portree ↗
    One of Scotland's oldest gatherings, held on The Lump above Portree harbour since 1877 — caber toss, tug-of-war, solo piping, Highland dancing, plus a hill race and yacht race. A procession leaves Somerled Square at 9:45; local events start 10:00, open events 11:45, running to ~5 PM. Tickets booked — 2 adults. Arrive early; parking and seating fill fast, and bring wet-weather gear.

    The heavy events on The Lump — the weight throw, the caber, and weight-over-the-bar, feet from the crowd.
    Solo piping and Highland dancing run alongside the field events all afternoon.
    Isle of Skye scenery — the Storr, a short drive from Portree.

    From Portree, ~30 min west toward Dunvegan for the evening's dinner reservation. Time to freshen up, or wander Dunvegan village, before the table.
  • 8:30 PM
    The Three Chimneys — Fine Dining ↗
    Booked — ref BYHQGFGW, table for 2, 20:30. One of Skye's two Michelin-recognised tables, in a converted 18th-century crofter's cottage at Colbost. Dietary requirements need 48 hrs' notice by emailing eatandstay@threechimneys.co.uk (no vegan or dairy-free options catered). Restaurant direct line: 01470 511258. Not driving? Dunvegan Taxi (07584 191131) serves the restaurant — worth booking early, they fill up fast. Cancel with at least 24 hrs' notice — a card is held to secure the table and a late cancellation/no-show is charged £130–£150pp.
    Navigation: there are two restaurants under The Three Chimneys name — set Google Maps/Sat Nav to postcode IV55 8ZT specifically to avoid a mix-up.
    Late return: Colbost is ~30 min beyond Portree, and Glengarry is already ~2.5 hr from Portree — so it's roughly a 3 hr drive back after dinner. A tasting-menu dinner finishing ~11 PM could mean arriving back at Glengarry around 2 AM. Worth deciding in advance whether to make the drive that night, book the Dunvegan taxi both ways, or arrange a room nearer Skye for just that one night.
