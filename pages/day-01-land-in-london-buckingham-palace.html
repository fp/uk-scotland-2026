Land in London — Buckingham Palace

Base: The Waldorf Hilton, London · Aldwych, Covent Garden · check in 3 PM · Hotel info →

A grand Edwardian hotel on Aldwych, steps from Covent Garden and the West End theatres. Its Palm Court is the birthplace of the modern afternoon tea, and the wrought-iron canopied entrance is a Theatreland landmark in its own right.
