Paul works · Wife’s day: York

Base: Leeds · Paul: business · Wife: independent (day return by rail)

  • ~25 min
    York ↗
    A quick hop from Leeds. York Minster, the medieval Shambles, and a walk of the city walls; then the JORVIK Viking Centre and the free National Railway Museum.
    JORVIK requires pre-booked, timed tickets — there’s no walk-up admission. Book online at jorvikvikingcentre.co.uk before this day.
  • Tea
    Bettys, York
    The York branch, on St Helen’s Square — open since 1936. Expect a queue, and it’s worth it. (This is a different room from the original Harrogate branch on Day 5 — both are worth the visit, not a repeat stop.)
Eat

Lunch In York, before or after the Minster: Star Inn The City (riverside brasserie next to the Museum Gardens, AA Rosette) or The Ivy St Helen’s Square (steps from Bettys and the Minster).

Dinner Back in Leeds when Paul is free.
