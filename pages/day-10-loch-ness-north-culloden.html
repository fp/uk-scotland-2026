Day 10Mon · 3 Aug

Loch Ness north & Culloden

Base: Glengarry Castle Hotel

  • Morning
    Urquhart Castle ↗
    A sprawling medieval ruin on the shore of Loch Ness near Drumnadrochit, run by Historic Environment Scotland, with a visitor centre and one of the best loch viewpoints anywhere. Pre-book — parking is limited and it gets busy; boat cruises leave from nearby.
    Learn more →
  • Afternoon
    Culloden Battlefield ↗
    The windswept moor where the 1746 battle ended the Jacobite rising in under an hour. The National Trust visitor centre lays out both sides of the story, and clan grave-markers stand out on the field itself. Allow ~2 hrs. About 35 min past Urquhart along the loch and through Inverness.
    Learn more →
