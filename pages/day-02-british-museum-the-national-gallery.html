British Museum & the National Gallery

Base: London · a full day in the city, no day trip today.

  • ~9:30 AM
    Platform 9¾ & the Harry Potter Shop ↗
    Leave the hotel by ~9:10 AM for the ~15–20 min hop to King’s Cross. The trolley disappearing into the wall is free to visit and photograph — no ticket needed — plus the Harry Potter Shop right next to it (opens 9 AM Sundays). Budget ~30 min, then it’s a 15–20 min walk through Bloomsbury (or a quick Tube hop) on to the British Museum, with time to spare before the 11:20 AM entry.
  • 11:20 AM
    The British Museum ↗
    Swapped in for Arundel Castle, which isn’t open on any of the days you’re in London. Booked — order 10481267, 2 tickets, 11:20 AM entry, £10 total (incl. £5 donation). About a 15–20 min walk (or a couple of Tube stops) from the hotel — the Rosetta Stone, the Parthenon sculptures, and the Egyptian mummies are the highlights. Keep it to ~2.5 hrs (out by ~1:50 PM) to make the 2:15 tea reservation.
